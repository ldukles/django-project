Naturalist

A full CRUD django web application that allows users to upload and keep track of their natural history sightings and contribute what they've observed to the greater scientific community.

Users can upload nature sightings and add helpful information such as the location seen, date, scientific name, identifying features, and more. Creating a virtual database of information relevant to the study of the natural world!

Create an account, and enjoy!
